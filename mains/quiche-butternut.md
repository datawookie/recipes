# Butternut Quiche

## Ingredients

- 1 butternut
- onions
- garlic
- 200 g yellow cheese (grated)
- tub sour cream
- block of feta cheese (cubed)
- spinach
- 3 large eggs
- puff pastry

## Method

1. Peel and chop the vegetables. Roast with some oil.
2. Wash and steam the spinach. Squeeze out water.
3. Dump vegetables, yellow and feta cheese, sour cream and eggs into a bowl. Mix.
4. Spray quiche dish with Spray 'n Cook. Line with puff pastry.
5. Dump vegetable mixture into dish.
6. Bake on 170 °C for around 40 minutes until quiche is firm.
