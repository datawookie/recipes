# Best Vegetable Lasagne

## Ingredients

Roast vegetables:

- 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
- 3 red or yellow peppers, deseeded and cut into strips
- 1 courgette (about 300 g), quartered lengthways and chopped
- 1 large sweet potato, peeled and chopped
- 1 egg plant
- garlic cloves, chopped
- 2 T extra virgin olive oil
- salt and black pepper

Tomato layer:

- 1 T olive oil
- 2 x 400 g tins chopped tomatoes
- 2 t Italian seasoning (or dried oreganum)
- 1 vegetable stock cube, crumbled
- 200 ml water
- 100 g spinach leaves (optional)

Cheese sauce:

- 75 g plain flour
- 75 g butter, cubed
- 750 ml milk
- 200 g mature cheddar, grated
- salt and black pepper

Assembly:

- lasagne sheets

## Method

1. Heat oven to 180 °C.
2. Put vegetables on a large tray. Drizzle with olive oil, season, toss, then roast for about 30 minutes until tender and lightly browned.
3. While they roast, heat olive oil in a large saucepan.
4. Add tomatoes, Italian seasoning, stock cube and water. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring now and then.
5. For the cheese sauce, put flour, butter and milk in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk continuously until smooth and thick.
6. Stir about two-thirds of the cheese into the sauce. Season to taste.
7. Add roasted vegetables to the tomato sauce. Taste and season.
8. In a baking dish, layer up: one-third vegetable sauce, lasagne sheets; another third veg sauce, lasagne sheets; just under half the cheese sauce; remaining vegetables sauce; final lasagne layer; remaining cheese sauce.
9. Scatter the reserved cheese on top.
10. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden, bubbling and the pasta is soft. Let it stand for 5 minutes before serving.
