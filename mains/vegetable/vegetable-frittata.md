# Vegetable Frittata

## Ingredients

- 4 potatoes
- 2 onions
- 1 red pepper
- 1 yellow pepper
- 1 butternut
- garlic
- spinach (optional)
- 6 large eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup cheese
- herbs

## Method

1. Peel the vegetables and chop into small pieces. Roast at 180 Â°C.
2. Steam the spinach.
3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cheese, salt, and pepper.
4. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables. Stir gently to distribute the eggs and vegetables evenly.
5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the top is slightly golden.
