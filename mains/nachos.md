# Nachos

## Ingredients

* 1 tbsp olive oil
* 1 onion, finely chopped
* 250 g beef mince
* 400 g tin black beans, drained
* 200 g lightly salted corn tortilla chips
* 150 g cheddar, grated
* 100 g cherry tomatoes, quartered
* 4 spring onions, finely chopped
* coriander, to serve (optional)
* sliced chillies, to serve (optional)
* lime wedges, to serve
* soured cream, to serve
* salt
* black pepper

These spices can be replaced by a spice blend like fajita spice.
  
* 1 tsp hot smoked paprika
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 0.5 tsp chilli powder

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 180 °C fan / 200 °C conventional.
2. Warm the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 3-5 minutes, until softened and translucent.
3. Increase the heat, then add the beef and spices. Cook for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beef is browned.
4. Reduce the heat to medium, stir in the black beans and cook until heated through. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Spread a layer of tortilla chips over the base of an ovenproof dish or baking tray. Top with half the beef mixture, half the cheddar and half the cherry tomatoes.
6. Add another layer of tortilla chips, then top with the remaining beef mixture and cheddar.
7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until the cheese has melted.
8. Scatter over the remaining cherry tomatoes, spring onions, coriander and sliced chillies, if using. Serve hot with lime wedges, guacamole and soured cream.
