# Guacamole

## Ingredients

* 1 large ripe tomato
* 3 very ripe avocados
* juice of 1 large lime or lemon
* handful of coriander, leaves and stalks chopped
* 1 small red onion, finely chopped
* 1 red or green chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
* salt
* black pepper

## Method

1. Chop the tomatoes and put in a blender.
2. Scoop out the avocado flesh and add to the blender.
3. Add the lime juice, coriander, red onion and chilli. Season with salt and black pepper.
4. Blend.
5. Refrigerate until serving.
