# Cauliflower Cheese & Bacon Quiche

## Ingredients

  * 225 g plain flour, plus extra for rolling
  * 140 g cold unsalted butter, roughly cubed
  * 1 large egg yolk
  * 2-3 tbsp ice-cold water
  * 1 small cauliflower, broken into small florets
  * 100 g thick-cut unsmoked streaky bacon, roughly chopped
  * 3 large free-range eggs
  * 284 ml double cream
  * 175 g cheese, finely grated (use a mix of parmesan, cheddar, gruyère or emmental)
  * 2 tbsp snipped chives
  * nutmeg
  * salt
  * black pepper

## Method

  1. To make the pastry, pulse the flour with a pinch of salt in a food processor. Add the butter and pulse until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  2. Mix the egg yolk with 2 tbsp ice-cold water. Add to the flour and butter, then process until the pastry comes together. Add a little more water only if needed.
  3. Shape the pastry into a thick disc, wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll the pastry out thinly and use it to line a 23 cm round tart tin, about 4 cm deep. Trim the edges and chill for another 30 minutes.
  5. Heat the oven to 170 °C fan / 190 °C conventional / gas 5. Line the pastry case with foil and baking beans, then blind bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Remove the foil and beans, then bake for another 5-7 minutes until the pastry is dry and lightly golden.
  7. Boil the cauliflower in salted water for 5 minutes, then drain well and leave to cool.
  8. Cook the bacon gently in a cold non-stick frying pan for 7-8 minutes, until the fat has rendered and the bacon has coloured but is not crisp. Leave to cool.
  9. Beat the eggs with the cream, cheese, chives, grated nutmeg, salt and black pepper. Stir in the cooled cauliflower and bacon.
  10. Pour the filling into the pastry case. Bake for about 30 minutes, until the pastry is golden and the filling is just set.
  11. Leave to stand for 30 minutes before serving warm.
