# Shortbread Biscuits

## Ingredients

- 2 cup flour
- 2 T cornflour
- 1/4 t salt
- 3 oz icing sugar
- 1/2 lb margarine (used butter but required quite a lot more!)

## Method

1. Sift dry ingredients into bowl.
2. Grate in butter and then rub in.
3. Make roll and slice into rounds about 1 cm thick.
4. Bake at 150 Â°C for around 25 minutes.
