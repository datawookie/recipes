## Ingredients

- 280 g bread flour
- 120 g all purpose flour
- 260 g water (cold)
- 1.2 g yeast
- 10 g salt
- 8 g oil
- 8 g sugar

## Method

1. Dump dry ingredients in a bowl.
2. Add water and oil. Stir in.
3. Flop out onto a surface and knead unti it has an even consistency. Don't over-knead!
4. Put into an oiled container with a lid.
5. Leave on the shelf for a day, opening every few hours and pull edge of dough over the centre (stretch and fold).
6. Put it into the friedge for another day, taking it out to do the same treatment every few hours.
7. Ball the dough a few hours before making the pizza.
