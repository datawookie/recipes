# Steam Pudding

## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup sugar
- 6 T milk
- 100 g butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup flour
- 0.5 t baking powder
- golden syrup

## Method

1. Cream butter and sugar.
2. Add egg then flour, baking powder and milk.
3. Grease a small bowl.
4. Add 2 T goldern syrup to the bowl.
5. Spoon in pudding mixture.
6. Steam for 2 hours.
7. Serve with syrup or custard.
