# Fattoush Salad

## Ingredients

* 2 pitas, torn into bite-sized pieces
* 3 T extra virgin olive oil, for crisping the pitas
* 2 t sumac, divided, plus extra to serve
* 1 heart of romaine lettuce, chopped
* 1 English cucumber, halved, deseeded and chopped or sliced into half moons
* 5 Roma tomatoes, chopped
* 5 green onions, white and green parts chopped
* 5 radishes, thinly sliced
* 2 cups fresh parsley leaves, chopped
* 1 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped (optional)
* salt
* black pepper

## Dressing

* juice of 1 lemon, or 1 1/2 limes
* 80 ml extra virgin olive oil
* 1 tbsp pomegranate molasses (optional)
* 1 tsp sumac
* 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1/8 tsp ground allspice
* salt
* black pepper

## Method

1. Heat 3 tbsp olive oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Add the torn pita and fry, tossing often, until golden and crisp.
2. Drain the pita on kitchen paper, then season with salt, black pepper and some of the sumac.
3. Put the lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, green onions, radishes, parsley and mint, if using, into a large bowl.
4. Whisk the lemon juice, olive oil, pomegranate molasses, if using, sumac, cinnamon, allspice, salt and black pepper together in a small bowl.
5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
6. Add the crisp pita, toss again, then taste and add more sumac, salt or black pepper if needed.
7. Serve immediately, while the pita is still crisp.
